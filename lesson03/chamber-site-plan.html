Site Name: Vitoria Chamber of Commerce

The name "Vitoria Chamber of Commerce" was selected to represent our local community's business and economic interests.

Site Purpose

1. To promote local businesses and economic growth in Vitoria-Gasteiz.

2. To provide resources and information for new entrepreneurs.

Scenarios

1. What events are scheduled this month to promote business-to-business networking?

2. Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

3. What has been the recent population growth in Vitoria-Gasteiz?
